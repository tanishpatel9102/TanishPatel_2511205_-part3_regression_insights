# Model Comparison

| Required Item             | Simple Regression Model                                                                                                              | Multiple Regression Model                                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Model Name**            | Simple Linear Regression                                                                                                             | Multiple Linear Regression                                                                                                                                                       |
| **Variables Used**        | **Dependent Variable:** Monthly Sales<br>**Independent Variable:** Marketing Spend                                                   | **Dependent Variable:** Monthly Sales<br>**Independent Variables:** Footfall, Average Discount %, Customer Rating, South, East, West (North is the reference category)           |

| **Business Usefulness**   | Explains the relationship between marketing spend and monthly sales but provides limited insight because only one predictor is used. | Provides a more comprehensive understanding of monthly sales by considering multiple business factors simultaneously, making it more useful for business decision-making.        |
| **Limitations**           | Does not account for other factors affecting sales such as customer behaviour, discounts, or regional differences.                   | Does not capture all external influences such as competition, seasonality, economic conditions, or promotional campaigns. Some variables are also not statistically significant. |

## Conclusion

The **Multiple Linear Regression** model is selected as the final model because it has a much higher R-squared value (75.20%) than the Simple Regression model (16.72%), indicating stronger explanatory power. It incorporates multiple business variables and provides better insights for predicting monthly sales and supporting business decisions.
